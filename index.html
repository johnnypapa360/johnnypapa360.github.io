Contract Audit

Contract audits in Dex-AI involve an in-depth analysis of the project's smart contract. It verifies if the contract is renounced, which signifies that no changes can be made to it and adds an extra layer of security for investors. The audit also inspects the tax and liquidity aspects, ensuring fair tokenomics and assessing the project's stability.
